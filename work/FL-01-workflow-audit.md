## Three Target Tasks (for reuse in FL-02–FL-04)

### Target Task 1: Writing code for my projects (Delegate to AI with review)
Done well means: AI's first draft runs with 2 or fewer bugs I have to fix myself, 
AND I can explain what every line does before I commit it.

### Target Task 2: Self-studying AI/ML, algorithms through MLOps (Collaborate with AI)
Done well means: after a session, I can explain the concept out loud without 
looking at the chat, AND I've written down at least one question the AI's 
explanation didn't fully resolve.

### Target Task 3: Making study notes for exams (Collaborate with AI)
Done well means: my notes are short enough to review in under 15 minutes, 
AND when I quiz myself from them a day later I get at least 80% right 
without re-reading the source material.
